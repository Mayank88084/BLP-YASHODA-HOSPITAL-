Who We Are

A hospital group designed to support treatment, consultation, and follow-up care.

BLP YASHODA Hospital and Research Centre is a healthcare institution in Gonda, Uttar Pradesh focused on accessible treatment, reliable physician support, and patient confidence at every stage of care.

The hospital network has been shaped to make healthcare approachable, with clear branch access, direct contact support, and clinical guidance that helps patients and families make informed decisions.
